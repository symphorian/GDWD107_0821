Controls:

Use the mouse to move the character around the world.

Click the mouse to jump. You can even jump in the air.

(Note: I am aware that when you click the mouse a lot, the level
tester glitches. That is because I have yet to set velocity limits to
the character, so if you keep clicking, the character's velocity may
approach infinity... Please don't do that to the poor duck!)
