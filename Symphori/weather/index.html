Weather
Status: Ongoing (last updated April 2011)

Description:
I don't really need anything fancy in a weather app; I just want to know at a glance the highs and lows for the next couple days, and what I'll see if I look up at the sky. And so, I created my own little weather app.

The app's nothing too complicated; it just uses an AS3/PHP combination to bypass some security sandbox issues. Even so, it's useful and kinda cute, so I put it up in case someone else finds it useful as well.
