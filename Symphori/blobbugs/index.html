BlobBugs
Status: Ongoing (last updated June 2011)
 

Description:
The BlobBugs originally stemmed from my random curiosity if I could recreate the floor movements of the Floods, an enemy in Kingdom Hearts: Birth by Sleep.

After successfully mimicing their movements, I expanded the experiment into a class that can be used for pretty much whatever you want: bugs, fish, ghosts, and more. I've used them in the blink demo, but the demo here is fun to play with as well. They look different in the day than they do at night, so come back at another time of day to see a different type of BlobBug.
