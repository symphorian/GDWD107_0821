Light Lavalamp
Status: Ongoing (last updated December 2010)
 

Description:
This relatively simple project doesn't actually bear any physical resemblance to a lavalamp, but the ever-shifting, never-quite-the-same shafts of light somehow make it hard to look away. The original intent was to mimic the effect of light streaming through the clouds or from the surface to the ocean floor, but more pressing matters forced me to temporarily abandon the project.

The demo was originally part of an experimental sidebar for my website before I had focused on a theme, so that is why the lights are oriented more to the left than the right.
