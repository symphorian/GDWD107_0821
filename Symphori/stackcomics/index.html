Stack Comics
Status: Ongoing (last updated June 2010)

Description:
Stack Comics were my answer to Scott McCloud's question: "What else can we do with comics?" Most traditional comics work in two dimensions, with panels expanding along the x- and y-axes; my idea is that panels can also extend in the direction of the z-axis, or more simply put, you can stack them. This idea maintains the gaps of time and space between panels that allow the viewer to use their imagination to bridge panels together, but also allows for additional creative opportunities for the creator, such as 'knocking over' the stack for a flashback or stacking new panels around a central panel that is key to what is happening.
