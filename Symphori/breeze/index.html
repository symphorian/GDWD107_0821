Breeze
Status: Completed Spring 2009

Description:
Breeze is less of a game and more of a toy. Up to two players can play at once on a keyboard using the arrow keys and the 'asdw' keys. The longer an object has been up in the air, the higher the chance that more objects of the same kind will appear. When a certain number of the same object appear in the same area, that area of the environment will change. But the more objects on the screen, the higher the chance a breeze will come and blow them all away.

Breeze is intentionally rather wide so that it is difficult for a single player to cover the whole area by themselves, hopefully encouraging players to play in pairs. My apologies if your screen resolution is not high enough to accomodate the entire game, but Breeze was designed to be played and displayed on a high resolution screen or projector.

Breeze was one of my first ventures into the realm of art and interactivity; the swf was created to give users a tool to enable self-expression through sight and sound, but the art piece itself is the unique combination of the two mediums generated through users' participation.
