Sammy the Superscaler in 'The Superscaler Sorting Factory'
 

Description:
"Sammy in 'The Superscaler Sorting Factory'" was my second educational game created for Professor R.P.H. Chang of the Materials Research Institute at Northwestern University over the summer of '09. This game, along with its precursor, was meant to supplement one of their teaching modules about size and scale.

Game Background:
Sammy the Superscaler is an animated character I created to introduce middle school students to the concepts of size and scale. In Sammy’s world, it is the job of a Superscaler to patrol various worlds and the scales found within them. Sammy’s patrol carries him to a planet very similar to ours and eight separate scales found within, classified by metric prefixes. After his patrols are complete, Sammy drops off his collection at the Superscaler Factory.

The goal of this game is to sort the objects and send them back to the appropriate scales where they belong.
