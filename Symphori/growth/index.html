Growth
Status: Ongoing (last updated January 2011)
 

Description:
My goal for this project was to mimic plant growth by using random numbers somewhat cleverly. The growth demo can generate some surprisingly natural-looking results at times; however, I think some real botanical research will be needed to further advance the project.

This project evolved from the BlobBugs after another random thought piqued my curiosity, this time to see if I could animate plant growth realistically. I'll make the demo more interactive once I find some free time, but for now, you have to refresh the page to see the plants grow differently.
