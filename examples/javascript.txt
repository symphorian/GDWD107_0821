Javascript -> NOUNS, ADJECTIVES, VERBS


Spot, sit.                    spot.sit();

Spot, speak!                  spot.speak();

Spot, say "Hello"!            spot.speak("Hello");

Spot's nickname is "Spotty".  spot.nickname = "Spotty";

That dog is Spot.             let thatDog = spot;

That dog, play dead.          thatDog.playDead();
